Stars Falling to Earth

On the left-hand side, you'll see an image from the NASA Image of the Day archive. Do you like it? Generate a color palette based on the colors shown in the image by clicking the "Extract color palette" button. The first one is already done for you, but do you want to see a different NASA image? No problem! Just hit the randomize button or choose a date to pick an image from a specific date in time. If you are amazed by the colors in the image, generate the color palette and if you want to, copy the hex codes that make up the palette! But let's take it a step further! Is there a color from that palette you particularly love? Click on it and on the right hand side you'll get an image from the Europeana database containing that color! Go ahead and try it!

Click on "Randomize" or "Search" to see a new NASA image!

Click on one of the squares in the color palette to get an image from Europeana using that color!
